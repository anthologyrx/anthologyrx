The Science

The master antioxidant your body was already making.

Glutathione is a tripeptide composed of glycine, cysteine, and glutamate. It is synthesized in every cell of the body and serves as the central hub of antioxidant defense, detoxification, and immune regulation.

Its primary roles include neutralizing free radicals, regenerating vitamins C and E, supporting liver detoxification pathways, and maintaining mitochondrial integrity. Glutathione depletion is associated with accelerated cellular aging, immune compromise, and increased susceptibility to oxidative damage.

Oral supplementation has limited bioavailability due to enzymatic breakdown in the gut. Injectable delivery bypasses this limitation entirely, achieving systemic concentrations that oral supplementation cannot reliably produce.

Clinical Applications

What it does, and why it matters.

Glutathione supports multiple systems simultaneously. These are the primary areas of clinical interest in physician-supervised protocols.

O

Cellular Antioxidant Defense

Neutralizes reactive oxygen species and free radicals that accumulate with age, stress, and environmental exposure. Supports baseline cellular integrity across multiple organ systems.

L

Liver and Detoxification Support

Central to Phase II hepatic detoxification. Glutathione binds to toxins and heavy metals, facilitating their excretion. Depleted levels impair the liver's capacity to process environmental compounds.

I

Immune Regulation

Modulates the proliferation and function of T-cells and NK cells. Immune cells are particularly sensitive to oxidative stress and require adequate glutathione for optimal activity.

M

Mitochondrial Function

Protects mitochondrial membranes from oxidative damage and supports the electron transport chain. Mitochondrial glutathione depletion is a recognized contributor to cellular energy decline.

S

Skin and Pigmentation

Inhibits melanin synthesis pathways, supporting skin clarity and evenness. Injectable glutathione has been studied for its effects on skin tone and photoprotection in clinical settings.

R

Recovery and Resilience

Supports post-exercise recovery by clearing exercise-induced oxidative byproducts. Relevant for individuals with high physical demand, frequent training, or extended recovery periods.
