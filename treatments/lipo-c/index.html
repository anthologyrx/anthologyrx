The Science

Four compounds. One protocol. A liver that can keep up.

Lipo-C is a lipotropic injection combining Methionine, Inositol, Choline, and Cyanocobalamin (Vitamin B12). Each compound plays a distinct role in hepatic metabolism, cellular signaling, and energy production. Together they support the liver's capacity to process lipids and sustain normal metabolic function.

Lipotropic agents work by supporting the methylation pathways and phospholipid synthesis required for healthy hepatic function and energy utilization. These are not stimulants. They are cofactors your metabolism depends on.

Injectable delivery ensures direct bioavailability, bypassing the absorption variability associated with oral lipotropic supplements. The compounded formulation is prescribed by a licensed physician and prepared to exact specifications by our pharmacy partner.

What's Inside

The formula, explained.

Each compound in the Lipo-C formulation has a specific metabolic role. This is not a proprietary blend. These are well-characterized compounds with established mechanisms of action.

Methionine

Essential Amino Acid

An essential sulfur-containing amino acid involved in the synthesis of carnitine, which transports fatty acids into the mitochondria for oxidation. Supports methylation reactions and liver detoxification pathways.

Inositol

B-Vitamin Derivative

A carbocyclic sugar involved in insulin signaling and lipid transport. Supports the metabolism of fat by the liver and plays a role in cellular membrane integrity and neurotransmitter signaling.

Choline

Essential Nutrient

Required for the synthesis of phosphatidylcholine, a structural component of cell membranes and the primary molecule responsible for transporting fat out of the liver. Choline deficiency is directly associated with hepatic fat accumulation.

Cyanocobalamin (B12)

Water-Soluble Vitamin

Essential for red blood cell formation, neurological function, and DNA synthesis. Supports energy metabolism at the cellular level and works synergistically with the other lipotropic compounds in this formula.
