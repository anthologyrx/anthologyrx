The Process

Same clinical rigor. Every protocol.

  1. 01

    Complete Your Assessment

    A structured 3-minute intake — health history, current medications, goals. No video call. No waiting room. Complete on your schedule.

  2. 02

    Provider Review

    A board-certified MD reviews your intake within 48 hours. They evaluate candidacy, flag contraindications, and issue your prescription if approved.

  3. 03

    Pharmacy Dispatch

    Your medication ships from a licensed 503A/B pharmacy. Temperature-controlled packaging, delivered directly to your door.

    Monthly provider check-ins, dosing adjustments based on your response, and direct messaging access. Your protocol evolves as you do.
