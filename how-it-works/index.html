01
Intake

Complete your intake assessment.

The process starts with a structured health questionnaire — approximately 10 minutes. It covers your medical history, current symptoms, goals, and any medications or conditions relevant to the protocol you're considering. It is not a marketing survey. It is the clinical intake a provider needs to make an informed prescription decision.

You'll answer questions about your hormone health, metabolic markers, energy levels, body composition concerns, and lifestyle. Everything you share is protected under HIPAA and reviewed only by licensed clinical staff. No data is used for advertising. No intake is shared with third parties.

If you're unsure which protocol is right for you, the assessment is designed to surface that. You don't need to arrive with a diagnosis — you need to arrive honestly.

No office visit required. The entire intake is completed online, on your schedule, from any device. Most patients complete it in under 10 minutes.

02
Review

A licensed provider reviews your case.

Your intake is reviewed by a contracted, board-certified physician — not an algorithm, not an AI-generated approval, not a rubber stamp. A real provider reads your clinical history, evaluates whether you meet the criteria for the requested protocol, and makes an independent medical judgment.

Where additional information is needed — labs, clarifying questions, or alternative protocol considerations — the provider will request it before issuing a prescription. If a protocol isn't appropriate for your clinical profile, you'll be told that directly, with explanation. AnthologyRX does not prescribe to everyone who applies. That's not a limitation. That's the point.

In eligible markets, provider review is completed in under 48 hours. You'll receive a notification when your case has been reviewed and a decision has been made.

Real doctors, real decisions. Every prescription issued through AnthologyRX is the result of an independent clinical review by a licensed physician. No protocol ships without provider approval.

03
Dispatch

Your protocol ships from a licensed pharmacy.

Upon approval, your prescription is transmitted directly to our compounding pharmacy partner — a licensed 503B outsourcing facility. 503B designation means the pharmacy operates under FDA oversight for sterile compounding, with manufacturing standards that exceed what standard retail pharmacies are required to meet.

Your medication is compounded to the exact specifications in your prescription and shipped in temperature-controlled packaging, direct to your door. No pharmacy pickup. No retail middleman. The supply chain between your provider's prescription and your front door is short, controlled, and documented.

Compounds prepared through AnthologyRX are not FDA-approved drug products — they are compounded medications, prepared by a licensed facility for individual patient use under a valid prescription. This distinction matters, and we explain it clearly so you know exactly what you're receiving.

503B accredited. Our pharmacy partner meets FDA-overseen sterile compounding standards — a higher bar than most retail pharmacies are held to. Your medication is prepared, tested, and shipped under those standards.

04
Ongoing Treatment

Your protocol evolves as you do.

Longevity medicine is not a one-time prescription. It is an ongoing clinical relationship. After your first shipment, your case remains active — providers monitor your progress, review labs at appropriate intervals, and adjust your protocol as your physiology responds and your goals evolve.

Monthly check-ins are built into the process. If you're experiencing side effects, seeing strong results, or want to discuss adding or modifying a protocol, your care team is accessible. No starting over. No re-explaining your history to a new provider each time.

Your subscription renews on a monthly basis and can be paused or cancelled at any time. Dosage refinements, prescription renewals, and lab orders are handled through your patient portal — no separate appointments required.

This is not a supplement subscription. It is an ongoing clinical protocol, managed by real providers, with the flexibility to evolve with you. The goal is not compliance. It is results.
