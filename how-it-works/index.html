How It Works

From intake to results — in four steps.

No waiting rooms. No gatekeeping. A board-certified physician reviews every intake within 48 hours. Your protocol ships from a licensed 503B pharmacy — straight to your door.

See If You Qualify → View Protocols
01
Step 01

Complete your medical assessment.

The AnthologyRX intake is a structured medical questionnaire — not a generic sign-up form. It covers your health history, current medications, goals, and any conditions that affect protocol eligibility. The whole thing takes under three minutes. No video call required. No waiting room. No scheduling.

Your answers are reviewed by a licensed physician — not an algorithm. Every response is evaluated in the context of your full health picture. If there are any contraindications or questions that require clarification, your provider will reach out before issuing any prescription.

This is the same clinical standard used by major telehealth platforms — built for thoroughness, designed for speed.

  • Under 3 minutes to complete
  • No video call required in most states
  • Health history, medications, and goals reviewed
  • All data is HIPAA-compliant and encrypted

Completing the intake does not constitute medical advice and does not guarantee a prescription. A licensed physician reviews every submission individually.

02
Step 02

A board-certified physician reviews your intake.

Within 48 hours of submitting your intake, a licensed physician reviews your full response set. They evaluate your candidacy against clinical eligibility criteria for your selected protocol, flag any contraindications, and make the prescribing decision.

AnthologyRX works with a network of board-certified physicians licensed in your state. Your provider isn't a generalist — they specialize in the specific protocol category you're pursuing. They know the research, they know the risks, and they're accountable to the same medical board standards as any in-person clinician.

If approved, your prescription is issued directly to our 503B pharmacy partner. If not approved, you are not charged — and your provider will often recommend an alternative protocol that may be a better fit.

  • Board-certified MD licensed in your state
  • 48-hour review window (often faster)
  • No charge if not approved
  • Provider may recommend alternative protocols

03
Step 03

Your medication ships from a licensed 503B pharmacy.

AnthologyRX dispenses all medications through a licensed 503B compounding pharmacy — the same regulatory standard used by hospital systems and major healthcare providers. 503B pharmacies operate under FDA oversight and are required to meet Current Good Manufacturing Practice (cGMP) standards. This is not a gray market. This is clinical-grade compounding.

Your medication is prepared, packaged, and shipped in temperature-controlled conditions. Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days from prescription approval. Recurring shipments are dispatched automatically on your protocol schedule — no reordering required.

Every shipment includes your dosing instructions, titration schedule, and injection guidance (for injectable protocols). If you have any questions about your medication, your care team is accessible via direct message.

  • Licensed 503B compounding pharmacy
  • FDA-overseen, cGMP-standard manufacturing
  • Temperature-controlled shipping, 3–7 business days
  • Automatic recurring dispatch each billing cycle — no reordering required. Pause or cancel anytime.

04
Step 04

Your protocol evolves with you.

A prescription isn't the end of the relationship — it's the beginning. Every AnthologyRX protocol includes monthly provider check-ins where your physician reviews your progress, adjusts your dosing if needed, and answers any clinical questions you have.

Between check-ins, you have direct messaging access to your care team. If you experience a side effect, have a question about your protocol, or want to discuss adding a second protocol — your provider is reachable. Not a chatbot. Not a generic support ticket. A licensed clinician who knows your history.

As you progress, your protocol can be adjusted: dose increases or decreases, transitions to maintenance dosing, stacking with complementary protocols, or pausing if life requires it. The goal is a sustainable outcome — not a subscription you're locked into.

  • Monthly provider check-ins included
  • Direct messaging access to your care team
  • Dosing adjustments based on your response
  • Cancel or pause anytime — no penalties

Our Standard

The same clinical rigor as an in-person practice.

Every provider in the AnthologyRX network is board-certified and licensed in the state where they practice. They are independent clinicians — not employees — held to the same standards as any physician you'd see in person.

Our pharmacy partner operates under 503B outsourcing facility registration, which requires FDA inspection, cGMP compliance, and product testing for potency, sterility, and endotoxins. These are the same standards that supply medication to hospitals and surgical centers.

Your health data is protected under HIPAA. Every data transfer is encrypted. Our platform is built on infrastructure that meets federal healthcare data security standards.

We don't cut corners on the clinical side to offer a lower price. We cut overhead — no waiting rooms, no front desk, no commercial real estate. That's where the savings come from.
