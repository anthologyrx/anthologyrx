Questions worth asking.

Compounded medications are not FDA-approved as finished drug products — but are legally manufactured under 503B where permitted, and prescribed by licensed physicians following a clinical evaluation. The regulatory landscape for certain compounded medications is subject to ongoing developments. Our compliance team actively monitors these requirements, and we will notify affected patients if a medication's availability changes.

Our platform operates within the applicable federal and state legal frameworks for telehealth and compounded medications. Our medications are compounded by a licensed 503A and 503B pharmacy under FDA oversight and cGMP manufacturing standards.

All protocols are prescribed by US board-certified physicians through an independent contracted provider network. AnthologyRX facilitates the telehealth encounter — your physician reviews your intake, orders labs where indicated, and signs your prescription. Prescriptions are fulfilled by VialsRx, a licensed 503A and 503A and 503B pharmacy. 503B outsourcing facilities are FDA-registered and subject to federal Current Good Manufacturing Practice (cGMP) standards. 503A pharmacies compound pursuant to individual patient prescriptions.
