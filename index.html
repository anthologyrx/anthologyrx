All protocols are prescribed by US board-certified physicians through an independent contracted provider network. We partner exclusively with a 503B-accredited outsourcing facility — the highest standard for compounded medications. 503B facilities are FDA-registered and subject to federal Current Good Manufacturing Practice (cGMP) standards, providing substantially greater oversight than traditional 503A compounding pharmacies.
