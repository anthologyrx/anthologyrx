Questions

The ones worth asking.

AnthologyRX operates through a network of independent, state-licensed physicians. The compounded medications we facilitate are prepared by a DEA-registered, 503B-accredited pharmacy. Compounded medications are not FDA-approved as finished products — they are individually prescribed formulations, legal under federal and state telehealth regulations.

All protocols are prescribed by US board-certified physicians through an independent contracted provider network. AnthologyRX facilitates the telehealth encounter — your physician reviews your intake, orders labs where indicated, and signs your prescription. You can message your provider directly through the member portal.

We partner exclusively with a 503B-accredited outsourcing facility — the highest standard for compounded medications. 503B facilities are FDA-registered and subject to federal Current Good Manufacturing Practice (cGMP) standards, providing substantially greater oversight than traditional 503A compounding pharmacies.

AnthologyRX is not an emergency medical service. Your provider can adjust dosing, pause, or discontinue your protocol at any time.

All health data is handled under full HIPAA compliance. AnthologyRX maintains a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with all platform and storage partners. Your intake, lab results, and provider communications are encrypted at rest and in transit. We do not sell or share your health information.
