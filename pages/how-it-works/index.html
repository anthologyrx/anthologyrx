Process

Simple. Clinical. Delivered.

Provider-guided treatment from your first assessment to your front door. No clinic visit required.

01
Assessment

Complete a short online health assessment. We’ll ask about your health history, goals, and current medications. Takes about 10 minutes.

02
Provider Review

A licensed provider reviews your assessment within 48 hours. If additional information is needed, they’ll reach out directly. No video call required.

03
Prescription & Pharmacy

If approved, your prescription is sent to a licensed 503B or 503A compounding pharmacy. Your treatment is prepared and shipped to your address.

04
Monthly Delivery

Your treatment arrives monthly. Your provider monitors your progress. Adjust dosing, switch treatments, or cancel anytime.

Common questions

Do I need to visit a clinic?

No. The entire process is online — assessment, provider review, prescription, and delivery.

How long until I receive my treatment?

Most patients receive their first delivery within 7–10 business days of provider approval.

What if I’m not approved?

Your assessment fee is refunded if you are not approved for treatment.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Cancel anytime before your renewal date with no penalty.

Is this covered by insurance?

AnthologyRX does not bill insurance directly. FSA/HSA cards may be accepted depending on your plan.

Ready to start?

Complete your assessment and a provider will review your intake within 48 hours. No clinic visit required.
