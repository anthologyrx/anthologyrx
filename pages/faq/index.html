Getting Started
What is AnthologyRX?

AnthologyRX is a performance telehealth company offering provider-guided injectable treatments for fat loss, body composition, energy, and recovery.

How do I get started?

Complete the online health assessment. A provider will review your intake within 48 hours.

Who reviews my assessment?

A licensed prescribing provider reviews every intake. We do not use AI to approve or deny treatment.

Do I need a prior diagnosis?

No. You complete a health assessment and a provider determines eligibility.

Treatments
What treatments do you offer?

GLP-1 (semaglutide/tirzepatide), NAD+, MICC lipotropic injections, tesamorelin, and PT-141. All require provider approval.

Are these FDA approved?

Semaglutide and tirzepatide are FDA-approved as branded medications. The compounded versions prescribed through AnthologyRX are prepared by licensed 503B pharmacies under the FDA’s compounding framework. These are not FDA-approved end products. NAD+, MICC, tesamorelin, and PT-141 are compounded medications prescribed off-label or under compounding authority. Your provider will disclose the regulatory status of your specific treatment during intake.

Can I combine treatments?

Combining treatments is evaluated case by case by your provider. Do not combine treatments without provider guidance.

Pricing & Billing
What does it cost?

GLP-1 from $199/mo. NAD+ from $149/mo. MICC from $99/mo. Tesamorelin and PT-141: pricing disclosed during assessment.

Is there a contract?

No. Monthly subscription. Cancel anytime before your renewal date.

Does insurance cover this?

AnthologyRX does not bill insurance. FSA/HSA may be accepted depending on your plan.

Is there an assessment fee?

Yes. The assessment fee is refunded if you are not approved for treatment.

Medical & Safety
What are the side effects?

Side effects vary by treatment. GLP-1 commonly causes nausea, especially early in treatment. Your provider will review risks during intake. Results and side effect profiles vary by individual.

Is this safe?

All treatments are prescribed by licensed providers and fulfilled by licensed pharmacies. As with any prescription medication, risks exist. Your provider will review these with you.

What if I have other medications?

Disclose all medications during your assessment. Your provider reviews interactions before approving any treatment.

Delivery
How is my treatment shipped?

Directly from the compounding pharmacy to your address. Cold-chain shipping where required.

How long until I receive my order?

Typically 7–10 business days after provider approval.

Can I adjust my dosing?

Yes. Reach out to your care team. Dosing adjustments are made by your provider.
